As spring training is now underway, I have a request. 
Please stop betting on baseball. Please. The game makes no sense. Stop throwing your money away. 
Stop trying to rationalize the game the way you can with other sports (I’m looking at you, basketball enjoyers). 

Over the last few years, I have seen several people around me try and make money quick. 
And what faster way to make money than gambling? Now, if you’re good at predicting athlete’s performances in the sport of your choosing, go ahead and do your thing and make your money. 
This message is not for you. But, if you’re just an average person trying to hit your parlays on DraftKings, stop betting on baseball if you aren’t familiar. 

I swear to God, the guys I know who watch basketball religiously and also bet on the games proceed to get a little cocky and think their analysis works on baseball. 
IT DOESN’T. Basketball has it’s own wacky advanced stats (RAPTOR, DARKO, and LEBRON come to mind). 
But baseball has a whole base of research dedicated to just advanced stats (Sabermetrics). 
And yet, regardless of how many stats you dive into, baseball sometimes just makes no sense.
Sometimes the best player can just get the yips and start slumping for seemingly no reason.
This is a sport where failing 7 out of 10 times for your whole careers would making you an All-Star player. 
Hell, at this point in baseball, you might honestly be a Hall of Fame player with those stats. 
And if you failed one less time, you would be one of the greatest players of all time. Nothing about this sport makes sense.

This entire sport hinges on superstition. The most common one is not to step on the damn baseline. 
Why would you toss money on a sport that follows the “step on a crack, break your momma’s back” mentality? 

Go bet on something else, like company earnings or something. 
At least something with the illusion of consistency. 
For everyone's sake, just go to the actual casino and put it all on black.
